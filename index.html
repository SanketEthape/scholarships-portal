General Category Scholarships

Economically Backward Class (EBC) Scholarship

Eligibility:

General category students from economically weaker families with an annual family income not exceeding ₹8 lakh.

Benefits:

Concessions on tuition fees and examination fees.

Documents Required:

  • Income certificate
  • Domicile certificate
  • College admission receipt
  • Marksheet of previous academic year

Deadline:

Generally between June and September.

Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Vastigruh Nirvah Bhatta Yojna (DTE)

Eligibility:

Maharashtra domicile, parents' income ≤ ₹8 lakh, enrolled in DTE-approved courses, staying in a hostel away from home.

Documents Required:

  • Domicile certificate
  • Income certificate
  • Hostel admission certificate
  • Bonafide student certificate
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Marksheet of previous academic year

Benefits:

₹30,000 (government hostels), ₹20,000 (private hostels).

Deadline:

Varies; check official DTE announcements.
